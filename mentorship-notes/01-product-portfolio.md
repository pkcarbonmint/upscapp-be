# Product Portfolio

### Available Products
- **Study Planner Product** - Self-managed Dynamic study planning
- **Personalized Mentorship - GS** - Individual guidance for General Studies [Prelims and Mains]
- **Personalized Mentorship - Optionals** - Individual guidance for Optional subjects [Mains]
- **Programme-based Mentorship - GS** - Structured program for General Studies [Prelims and Mains]
- **Programme-based Mentorship - Optionals** - Structured program for Optional subjects [Mains]
- **PCM-based Mentorship** - Specialized program for PCM students students  [Prelims and Mains] - -Suggested for second stage.

### Pricing Structure
| Product | Pricing Unit | Description |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Study Planner | Per Plan | Generated based on intake form and options |
| Personalized Mentorship | Per Mentorship-Month (3, 6, 9 or 12 months) | Individual mentor guidance |
| Programme-based Mentorship | Per Programme | Structured curriculum-based program |
| PCM-based Mentorship | Per PCM Programme | Specialized PCM curriculum |

### Study Plan Management
- **Study Planner**: Student manages their own plan with monthly rebalancing students  [Prelims and Mains] - -Suggested for second stage.
- **Mentorship Programs**: Mentor and student jointly manage the study plan  and update according to the progress.
- All products include an actively managed current study plan, dynamic in nature.
